MY STORY

OPERATIONS CONNOISSEUR & SERVICE DESIGN ENTHUSIAST

I am a San Francisco-based UX Designer looking to get my hands dirty in the world of product design. My background focuses on customer-facing roles where I have worked to understand the needs of customers in order to provide tangible solutions to solving their particular touchpoint. Thankfully, there were many overlaps of client service that carried over to user experience design. Earlier in 2017, I started turning my creative interest into seeking a new learning attainment at an immersive UX/UI apprenticeship, Bloc. Through this mentor-led program, I have equipped myself with the necessary skills to perform user research, strategize, and deliver solutions from a human-centered thinking.

With a deep appreciation for how the human mind works, I enjoy asking what makes us tick and why we do things the way we do. I am fascinated by the way technologies are changing the world and believe that clean and intuitive design is the hallmark of a company's success. What excites me the most about user experience design is that it gives the designer the opportunity to exercise their creative thinking to address everyday problems. As a junior designer, I am always looking for new toolsets and approaches to enhance my craftsmanship.

Apart from designing, you can find me staying on top of my fitness, hiking, attending festivals, and learning more about the nature of the mind and its relation to our everyday lives. Drop me a line and let's create something awesome together!
