ABOUT

Hello! My name is Ekkalux or Lux, committed to uncovering people's stories through the crafting of intuitive design solutions

Growing up, I have always had a deep appreciation for how the human mind works. Asking questions like what makes us tick and why do we do things the way we do, I was fascinated by the mystery in uncovering human behavior. The opportunity to listen, empathize, and learn inspire me to leap into the world of UX design.

My journey to become a UX designer started during my undergraduate career at UC Berkeley. I was involved in many different organizations, helping to design graphic materials - prints, ads, and newsletters. But I didn’t want to just design; I wanted to use my passion in design to help people with their problems and see the positive effect it has upon their situation. My passion to be a UX designer grew when I stumbled across an intensive UI/UX design program, Bloc. Through this program, I have equipped myself with the necessary skills to discover, plan, and deliver effective design solutions.

I am here to make the world a better place! With a strong affinity for creating products that bridge the gap between technology, health, fitness, and entertainment, I hope to strategize simple, yet user-centric solutions to solve complex problems. When I'm not designing, I enjoy hiking, traveling, hitting my personal records at the gym, having great conversations, and keeping up with the latest UX trends. I'm huge on personal development so I'm always open to new opportunities that help me maintain a good work-life balance.
