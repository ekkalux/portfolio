MOBILE APPLICATION | BLOC PROJECT | CASE STUDY

Today, defining and maintaining fitness is open to intrepretation. Whether that be health, sports, or recreational activity, Gymseeker seeks to assist users in finding the perfect gym to acheiving their fitness goals.

A Problem to Solve

Finding the right gym is different for everyone. Whether that is location, price or types of services offered, users must go through a rigorous research process before making a commitment. Applications such as Yelp does aid in the process of locating a gym, but it isn't comprehensive enough to cover the entirety of a gym's profile. Additionally, when expecations aren't met, users are not satisfied with their gym and may potentially neglect their fitness goals.

Seeking the Solutions

Gymseeker makes the process of finding a gym easier by streamlining the research and selection phase based on the users' needs. Users can search for gyms basd on location and view gym's profile before making a commitment to purchasing a membership, easing users in the decision-making process.

Discovery & Strategy Phase

Getting to know my Users

I initiated my research by crafting a 15 questions user survey in order to find out users' goals and pain points. Through this survey, I needed to learn the following:

  • User's fitness goals
  • What factor is important in selecting a gym
  • What frustrations do users have with their current gym
  • What stops users from going to the gym
  • What type of membership do they currently have

Research Insights

1. Buying a gym membership can be stressful for most users. With all the different factors presented (e.g community, overcrowding, lack of certain equipments), it is important that Gymseeker eases users in simplifying decision-making when choosing a gym.

2. People wants to meet their fitness goals, but find it hard to do so when they are met with pain points like being too tired from work or the gym is too far to make the trip.

Who are my Audiences

To gain a better understanding of what specific features were desired and what kind of people are likely to user Gymseeker, I compiled my research to create three different user personas: Avid gym-goer, occasional gym-goer and amateur gym-goer. These personas will guide me to design an intuitive flow for Gymseeker.
